BlockMiner Tutorial (Full Guide)

This page explains, step by step, how to use BlockMiner, how each feature works, and how users can build earnings over time through mining activities, tasks, and reward systems.

1) Start Here: Account and First Login

  1. Create your account from the Register page with a valid email and secure password.
  2. Login and open your Dashboard to see your account status and mining overview.
  3. Use the left menu to access all modules: mining, rewards, wallet, games, social, and settings.

Security tip: always use a unique password and keep your session private on shared devices.

2) Core Earnings Logic: Mining Power

Your earnings are tied to your hash rate contribution in the platform. In simple terms, more active and optimized power can increase your share of rewards.

  • Dashboard: monitors your miner profile, account metrics, and current progression.
  • Mining Stats: displays block/reward information and network-level activity.
  • Ranking: compares users by power/performance so you can track your position.

3) Improve Performance: Shop, Inventory, Machines, Racks

  • Shop: purchase miners and upgrades available in the current economy.
  • Inventory: stores owned assets and allows item installation/removal when supported.
  • Machines: activate, upgrade, toggle, or remove machines according to your strategy.
  • Racks: organize machine slots and naming for better mining room management.

Practical strategy: combine active machines + proper upgrades to build stable long-term output instead of relying on only one reward source.

4) Extra Earnings Modules

  • Daily Check-in: complete the check-in flow each day to unlock recurring rewards.
  • Earnings / Faucet: claim faucet rewards following cooldown and participation rules.
  • Earnings / PTC: complete paid-to-click actions when available.
  • Shortlink: progress through shortlink steps for task-based rewards.
  • Auto Mining GPU: use GPU-related reward flow where enabled by your account/activity.
  • Games (Memory and others): complete game actions that can grant temporary hash boosts.

5) Wallet, Transactions, Withdrawals, and Swap

  • Wallet page: view balance, connect your external wallet (when required), and manage withdrawals.
  • Transactions: review deposits, withdrawals, statuses, and timestamps in your history.
  • Withdrawals: submit to your configured address and monitor processing states.
  • Swap: convert assets according to currently available swap options and rates.

Important: processing times and network confirmations can vary. Always verify destination address and network before confirming.

6) Growth Features: Referral, PTP, Events, Blog, Community

  • Referral: invite users and track referral activity/rewards based on platform rules.
  • PTP: use promotion/share flows where available to increase ecosystem participation.
  • Events: follow time-limited campaigns for seasonal or special bonuses.
  • Blog: read updates, strategy content, and release announcements.
  • Chat: communicate with other users in real time for tips and collaboration.

7) Settings and Account Control

  • Open Settings to update profile preferences and available account options.
  • Keep your data up to date to avoid access or payout issues.
  • Use Logout after sessions, especially on non-personal devices.

8) Practical "How to Earn" Path (Step by Step)

  1. Login daily and do Daily Check-in.
  2. Run available tasks in Faucet, PTC, and Shortlink.
  3. Use Shop + Machines + Inventory to increase your mining performance.
  4. Play reward-enabled games (such as Memory) to gain temporary boosts when available.
  5. Track progress in Mining Stats and Ranking, then optimize weak points.
  6. Manage funds in Wallet and request withdrawals responsibly.
  7. Use Referral and Events to add extra growth channels.

Transparency note: earnings depend on your activity level, available features, system rules, and current network/economy conditions. There is no guaranteed fixed income.
